Using Sneakoins in Your Projects

In this guide, we'll show you how to earn and use Sneakoins in your projects.

Notice: After the 1.7.1 update, a limit of 3,000 Sneakoins will be placed. Ensure you do not have purchases in a shop system for over 3,000 Sneakoins or a feature that gives over 3,000 Sneakoins.

Earning Sneakoins

Sneakoins can be earned through various activities within the Sneakon ecosystem, such as completing tasks, participating in events, or contributing to the community.

Using Sneakoins

To integrate Sneakoins into your projects, you can use the local storage API to store and retrieve the number of Sneakoins. Below are examples for setting and getting the amount of Sneakoins:

Setting Sneakoins

You can set Sneakoins in the case of earning Sneakoins from your game or losing Sneakoins from a Shop system.


localStorage.setItem("sneakon_coins", [amount to add]);

Getting Sneakoins

You can get Sneakoins in the case of checking if one has enough coins to buy something in a shop system.


var sneakoins = localStorage.getItem("sneakon_coins");

With these commands, you can manage the number of Sneakoins in your projects and enhance the user experience.
